# Step 1 - Directing Brief

## Working Title

3步上手，效率提起来

## Core Strategy

This is not a brand film. It is a short internal adoption piece.

The audience is front-line pharma sales, so the message must be immediately practical:

- what problem it solves
- what three actions they need to take
- what result they will feel right away

## Format Direction

Pure motion graphics only:

- no actors
- no office footage
- no fake AI persona
- no cinematic transitions
- no abstract "future tech" visuals

Instead use:

- clear typography
- app UI cards
- icon systems
- comparison states
- line motion and block transitions

## Visual Language

- Main tone: clean, internal, efficient, trustworthy
- Color direction: enterprise blue, teal, white, dark gray
- Shape language: cards, tabs, checkmarks, arrows, message bubbles
- Motion style: crisp, short, snappy, readable

## Communication Spine

Message order:

1. Daily work is fragmented and repetitive
2. This tool helps organize, summarize, and output faster
3. Use it in three simple steps
4. Start now in your internal workflow

## Success Standard

After 15 seconds, a sales rep should clearly understand:

- this is for daily work, not "tech experimentation"
- it can reduce repetitive admin effort
- getting started is easy

## Mandatory Avoidances

- no overhyped AI language
- no sci-fi look
- no vague concept shots
- no dense feature lists
- no long voiceover logic
